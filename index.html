About Me

Chetana (Veronica) Makeup Artist | Dreamer | Detail Lover

I’m Chetana, but you can call me Veronica. I come from the humble village of Khudunabari, Jhapa—with a heart full of dreams and hands born to create beauty. After graduating with my bachelor’s degree, I pursued one of the most advanced makeup training programs available, earning a world-class certification that sharpened my skills and deepened my passion.

Makeup isn’t just my profession—it’s my calling. I believe every face tells a story, and I’m here to highlight yours with care, creativity, and confidence. Whether it’s bridal glam, everyday elegance, or a bold transformation, I bring precision, warmth, and a smile to every session.

I’m known for my attention to detail, easygoing nature, and strong, supportive personality. I love connecting through polite, heartfelt conversation—and I never settle for less than your full satisfaction.

This is just the beginning. I’m here to make you feel seen, celebrated, and stunning. I promise: I’ll never disappoint.
